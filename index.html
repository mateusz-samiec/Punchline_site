How It Works

  1. Open Punchline and grant microphone/speech access.
  2. Choose built-in effects or import your own sounds.
  3. Assign voice keywords and shake triggers.
  4. Trigger cinematic sound effects in real time.

Local-First Privacy

Punchline is designed with a local-first approach. Your sound library and imported files stay on your device. No custom analytics backend is required for core app functionality.
